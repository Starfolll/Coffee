Latte

Alatteis a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. The term as used in English is a shortened form of the Italian caffè latte, which means "milk coffee". The word is also sometimes spelled latté or lattè in English with different kinds of accent marks, which can be a hyperforeignism or a deliberate attempt to indicate that the word is not pronounced according to the rules of English orthography.

Cappuccino

Acappuccinois an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, and is traditionally prepared with double espresso and steamed milk foam.

Americano

Caffè Americano or Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from traditionally brewed coffee. The strength of an Americano varies with the number of shots of espresso and the amount of water added. The name is also spelled with varying capitalization and use of diacritics: e.g., café americano.
